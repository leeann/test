Galeri NIH is a research-driven gallery space offering insights into the research work being carried out at the National Institutes of Health (NIH) Malaysia.

We are working to establish collaborative partnerships that will enable exciting interdisciplinary interactions between art, healthcare, and medicine. The gallery will also be a centre for curatorial exchange between NIH researchers, Ministry of Health staff, academicians, students and the general public.
